Polygons

These polygons show the time in the form yy:M:w:d:h:mm:ss, where M is the month, w is the week in the current month, and d is the day of the week.

: : : : : :

Blobs

The leftmost blob has a travelling wave that passes the top once per millennium. The following blobs show waves that pass once per year, day, hour, minute, and second, respectively.


The final blob has all six of the above waves on it.

Peaks

The highest peak moves from right to left once per millenium, the next highest once per year, once per day, once per hour, and once per minute.

Waves

Similar to the peaks, but using Bézier curves instead of sharp edges.
